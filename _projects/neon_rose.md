After his first mission as a _Protector_, Lexius Garrick's return home is interupted by a deadly goblin ambush! Help Garrick stop the ambushers as they search for something... or someone! What is the mysterious Neon Rose, and who sent these goblins to find it?

## What it is
Neon Rose is a proof of concept RPG for both the GameBoy classic and Color systems created using GB studio. This demo is adapted from the first "chapter" of the original D&D campaign from which the world of shadows was built from. The game is a fairly standard RPG compared to games of the time and has clear inspiration in Pokemon and Final Fantasy. The only flair I would say I've added is bringing the dice rolls that usually happen under the hood in RPGs and making that a core part of the game. This was largely inspired by the game's D&D origins (though doesn't use D&D's stats system).
